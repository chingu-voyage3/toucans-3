What is JurisBot

JurisBot is a professonial, scholarly Bot 🤖 who gives you legal advice on Slack! This way, you don't need to leave your beloved channel for inquiries.

Keep in mind though, while JurisBot is cute, he only dispenses the best kind of legal services - free. For that reason, and many more; if you have a serious legal issue, please consider clicking here.

Real legal requests shouldn't be handled through this bot.
